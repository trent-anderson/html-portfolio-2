Three Great Movies

These Are Three Great Movies I, Trent, Like

The Restoration

This is an amazing movie about Joseph Smith, with a focus on his experience seeing Heavenly Father and Jesus Christ. Missionaries of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints could tell you more about Joseph Smith.

Joseph Smith: Prophet of the Restoration

This is a glorious movie about Joseph Smith, including portions about his experience translating The Book of Mormon from metal plates into English. The Book of Mormon is available in many languages.

The Lion King

This is an amazing movie with powerful symbolism in connection with our identity as children of God. According to rogerebert.com, Roger Ebert gave this film three and a half out of four stars.
